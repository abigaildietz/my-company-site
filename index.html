Who We Are

NJ GreenFlow is an Engineering Management (EM) firm specializing in sustainable stormwater systems and flood resilience projects across New Jersey. We manage complex infrastructure programs by integrating civil, environmental, and municipal stakeholders, aligning schedules and budgets, and ensuring performance outcomes that support regulatory compliance and long-term community resilience.

How We Add Value to Engineering Management (Requirement 7)

Stakeholder Integration

  • Coordinate municipalities, agencies, designers, and contractors
  • Facilitate clear communication and decision-making
  • Manage scope changes and expectations

Regulatory & Compliance Management

  • Support NJDEP-related compliance planning and documentation
  • Track permit needs, reporting timelines, and inspection requirements
  • Reduce project delays and compliance risk

Risk, Cost, and Schedule Control

  • Reduce flood risk and infrastructure damage through structured delivery
  • Use lifecycle cost analysis to prioritize high-value green solutions
  • Apply risk registers, milestones, and performance metrics

Sustainable Project Delivery (Requirement 9)

  • Low Impact Development (LID) planning
  • Green roofs, rain gardens, bioswales, permeable pavement
  • Runoff reduction, groundwater recharge, and pollution control

Services (Requirement 8)

Our services include green infrastructure planning, stormwater compliance consulting, flood mitigation project management, lifecycle cost and risk analysis, and performance monitoring. We deliver projects using clear work breakdown structures, schedule baselines, earned value-style tracking, and stakeholder reporting aligned with sustainable outcomes.
