Who We Are

NJ GreenFlow is an Engineering Management firm specializing in sustainable stormwater systems and flood resilience projects across New Jersey. We manage complex infrastructure programs by integrating civil, environmental, and municipal stakeholders, aligning schedules and budgets, and ensuring performance outcomes that support regulatory compliance and long-term community resilience.

Our Services

Stakeholder Integration

  • Coordinate municipalities, agencies, designers, and contractors
  • Facilitate clear communication and decision-making
  • Manage scope changes and expectations

Regulatory & Compliance Management

  • Support NJDEP-related compliance planning and documentation
  • Track permit needs, reporting timelines, and inspection requirements
  • Reduce project delays and compliance risk

Risk, Cost, and Schedule Control

  • Reduce flood risk and infrastructure damage
  • Use lifecycle cost analysis to prioritize green solutions
  • Apply structured project controls and performance metrics

Sustainable Infrastructure Planning

  • Low Impact Development (LID) planning
  • Rain gardens, bioswales, permeable systems
  • Runoff reduction and groundwater recharge strategies

Example Project Concepts

Green Roofs

Installation of vegetated roofing systems to reduce runoff volume, improve insulation performance, and mitigate urban heat island effects while enhancing stormwater retention capacity.

Pervious Pavement Systems

Implementation of permeable surface infrastructure to increase groundwater recharge, reduce surface runoff, and minimize municipal drainage system overload during heavy precipitation events.
